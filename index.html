Self Love

What is Self Love? When we talk about ‘self loveʼ, what goes on in the minds of most people is all about the saying ‘love yourself before you love anotherʼ.
You canʼt give what you donʼt have right?. Itʼs very accurate but itʼs a lot deeper than that.
We all put smiles on our faces everyday, I mean itʼs very easy to pretend to be happy.
But what we all forget sometimes is that, we get accustomed to and addicted to things we love to do often.
Some of us go the extent of pretending to be happy that we actually forget what true happiness feels like,
sometimes we are unhappy without even knowing and we get used to unhappiness that it slowly becomes an addiction or a hobby,
it becomes who we are. I was unhappy for so many years donʼt get this the wrong way, some people might think itʼs because I
didnʼt have friends, but rather I had amazing friends so many I was practically a happy child so I thought,
you will hardly see me without smiles until one day I had to examine myself with sincerity,
you can lie to others but you can never lie to yourself. I thought about so many things;
the friends you meet (both the good and bad ones),the things we do just to fit in or just to be regarded or called a friend,
we tolerate peopleʼs crap,not because weʼre nice or weʼre weak but itʼs simply because of the lack of self love,
because we donʼt love ourselves enough so we donʼt place value on ourselves.
Itʼs very easy to post, but do you truly love yourself? Weʼre not talking about posting pictures on Instagram, I mean do you
truly love yourself to the extent that you wonʼt tolerate peopleʼs crap?

Do you truly love yourself to that extent where you feel you donʼt need to be loved by anybody but yourself?
Do you truly love yourself to the extent that you could stay months or years without any from of relationship except with your creator?
Don't get me wrong, we all need people at every stage of our journey on this planet, but when we don't meet such people, can we stay and be
comfortable in our skin and use that period to work on our relationship with God?
Do you truly love yourself to the extent of putting yourself before anyone or anything?
Do you truly love yourself to the extent where you never feel pressured to do something against your will?
Do you truly love yourself to the extent where you teach people how to treat you by deciding what you will and wonʼt accept?.
I heard someone saying “when you know who you are you donʼt act like everyone else”.
Examine yourself, self love is one of the greatest feeling on earth.
If you truly know what it means I know I do and am loving every moment of my life.
You deserve to feel that way too, I strongly encourage everybody to embark on the journey of self love.
Enough is enough you deserve the best. Never settle for less Never!!



Peace Ali Jackson
